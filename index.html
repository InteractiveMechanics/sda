SDA at a Glance

What is SDA?

The Surface Design Association is a national organization focused on inspiring creativity, encouraging innovation, and advocating for artistic excellence as the global leader in textile-inspired art and desing. Our mission is to promote awareness and appreciation of textile-inspired art and design through publications, exhibitions, and conferences.

Becoming a Member

If you love the textile arts, you already belong. We offer a range of member benefits, from our quarterly publications, monthly blogs and newsletters, our diverse community of makers, and opportunities for exposure and promotion online and acorss the nation.

The Journal

Surface Design Journal is a quarterly print an digital publication (available on all mobile devices) that comes with membership in Surface Design Association. Each issue of SDA Journal is designed aorund a theme — such as techniques, genres, aesthetic direcitons, or a part of the world, such as Africa.
